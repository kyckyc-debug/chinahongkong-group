About Us

CHINAHONGKONG GROUP is a leading intellectual property service company based in Hong Kong, providing trademark, industrial design, patent, and copyright registration services worldwide. Since our incorporation in 1999, we have helped clients in the Greater China Region and abroad to secure and protect their intellectual property rights in Asia and internationally.

Our mission is to deliver the most cost-effective IP protection solutions for our clients, offering package pricing (not time-based) that includes all official fees and professional service costs.

We are proud members of the International Trademark Association (INTA) since 2000. In Hong Kong, we consistently rank among the top five e-filers for new trademark applications.

Our Services

  • Trademark Registration (Hong Kong, China, Macau, Asia, Worldwide)
  • Patent Registration (including PCT, standard, and short-term patents)
  • Industrial Design Registration
  • Copyright Registration
  • Patent Specification Drafting (with in-house, China-qualified professionals)
  • IP Strategy Consulting
