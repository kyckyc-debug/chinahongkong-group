Profile

We are an intellectual property service company based in Hong Kong providing worldwide trademark, industrial design, patent and copyright registration services. Since our incorporation in 1999, we have been assisting our clients in the Greater China Region to secure protection for their intellectual property rights in Asia and worldwide.
We have also been assisting overseas clients, including foreign law firms, to secure protection for their or their clients' intellectual property rights in Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau and other Asian territories.

Mission

Our mission is to provide clients with the most cost-effective intellectual property protection solutions in view of the applicable laws and the best commercial interests of clients. We do not charge on a time basis.

Our Advantage

We charge a package price for each registration and this covers all official fees, our service fees and all related disbursements from filing up to issuance of the registration certificate. This is one of the main differences that distinguishes us from other agents and law firms in China and Hong Kong.

Expertise

We are also one of the few Hong Kong agents that have in-house patent specification drafting capability. Our patent professionals include China qualified patent attorneys who have passed the Chinese National Qualification Examinations for Patent Agents, and are specialised at handling various patent matters for overseas clients especially the PCT applications entering the national phase in China and the applications for standard and/or short-term patents in Hong Kong.

Recognition

We are a member of the International Trademark Association (www.inta.org) since 2000. In Hong Kong, we were among the top five e-filers of all new applications every year from 2007 up to the present.
